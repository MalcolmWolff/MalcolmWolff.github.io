About

I am a PhD student in the University of Washington Department of Statistics. My research is focused on robust machine learning algorithms focusing on generalizations of the least trimmed squares estimator. I also conduct applied statistical research at the Center for Education Data & Research (CEDR). I currently hold Bachelor of Science in mathematics and statistics at the University of Washington.
